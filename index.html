About Us

Established in 1996, G H Raisoni College of Engineering [GHRCE] is a premier Autonomous institution in the central India imparting a holistic technical education to the students residing not only in India but also international students. The institution has always been ranked amongst a well performing institution by National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF), MHRD, Government of India. Currently GHRCE is ranked 130th PAN-India in Engineering Discipline as declared by NIRF Ranking 2021 The institution has also been ranked prominently by India Today MDRA Survey and Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements [ARIIA]. 1st Rank in India at ARIIA-2021 among private / Self Financed College / institutions
